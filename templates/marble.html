Marble is gorgeous stones that offer classic beauty

Consisting primarily of calcite and dolomite or a combination of both minerals. Marbles are easy maintain and can be re polished if you need to bring shine back. It is a porous material so if strong stain material is spilled on marble, it is recommended to wipe it quickly so that the marble does not absorb the stain. There is enormous amount of waste generated in cutting the marble tiles. Inserting marbles in floors is very much time consuming. Marble is weak to acidic products, such as tomato vinegar and lemon.
